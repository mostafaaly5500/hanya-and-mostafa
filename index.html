Getting There

Crowne Plaza West Cairo Arkan sits in the vibrant Arkan Plaza complex in Sheikh Zayed City, right off the 26th of July Corridor. The venue is about a 38‑minute drive from Cairo International Airport and is close to numerous dining and shopping destinations. Use the interactive map below to plan your route.

Travel & Accommodation

Travel & Stay

We want your journey to be seamless. Here are some helpful tips on getting to and staying near our venue.

Hotels Nearby

  • Staybridge Suites – Connected to Arkan Plaza, offering spacious suites and complimentary breakfast.
  • Hilton Pyramids Golf – A short drive away, perfect for guests who want a resort experience.
  • Novotel Cairo 6th of October – Modern rooms with easy access to the 26th of July Corridor.

Transportation

  • Use ride‑hailing apps like Uber or Careem to reach the venue safely.
  • Taxis are readily available; ask for Arkan Plaza in Sheikh Zayed.
  • On‑site valet parking is available at Crowne Plaza for your convenience.

Parking

Arkan Plaza provides covered parking garages and valet service. Simply follow the signs for event parking and the attendants will direct you.
